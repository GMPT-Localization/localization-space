Arabic is the only gap. Symphony Creative Studio covers 18 of TTAM’s 19 languages — all except Arabic. This surfaces right at the new More tools → TikTok Symphony handoff: an advertiser running Ads Manager in Arabic lands on Symphony with no Arabic UI and falls back to a default.P0 · SA / UAE / EG
Two notes from the sheet itself: (1) the Shopify column is really the Shopify Channel App (SCA) — TikTok’s embedded sales-channel app inside Shopify Admin — and the cell links to its Starling localization project, not shopify.com.data quirk (2) The column header is “Platform & Interface”, confirming these 13 are the surfaces being localized.
